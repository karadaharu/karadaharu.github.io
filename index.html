RESEACH

CURRENT PROJECT -Population dynamics on immune cell development-

T cell, one of immune cells, gets matured step by step from immature cells in thymus. In this maturation process, only T cells which recognize antigens such as bacterium and don’t recognize cell from self by mistake, can proliferate and get matured through an interaction with thymic epithelial cells(TECs) and thus T cells which only attack enemy are selected. The molcullar mechanisim of the selection by TECs has been revealed so far, but we still have little knowledge on the relationship between the selection by TECs and the dynamics of T cell population. We are developing a mathematical model in which the time dependent numbers of cells are formulated by ordinary differential equation and inferring the relationship between the dynamics of T cell development and the selection by TECs.
